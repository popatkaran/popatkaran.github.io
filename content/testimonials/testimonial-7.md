We had a distributed system that had grown organically over five years. No single person understood all of it. Onboarding new engineers took months. Debugging production incidents required tribal knowledge that was leaving the company as people moved on.

He mapped the entire system in two weeks — dependencies, data flows, failure modes, and the undocumented assumptions baked into every service boundary. He produced documentation that became the foundation for every architectural decision we made for the next year, and a service catalogue that cut our onboarding time in half.
