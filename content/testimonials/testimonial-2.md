We engaged him as a fractional Tech Architect during a critical period — we were scaling from 50 to 200 engineers and our architecture was not keeping pace. He ran a full architectural review, identified the three coupling points that were slowing every team down, and designed the migration path that let us address them without stopping feature delivery.

What sets him apart is the ability to hold the full complexity of a system in his head while communicating it clearly to engineers, product managers, and executives simultaneously. That is not a common skill.
