He joined us as Staff Engineer at a moment when we had strong individual contributors but no shared engineering culture. Within three months of joining, every team was writing better code, having better design conversations, and shipping with more confidence. He introduced ADRs, a lightweight RFC process, and a code review standard that actually got followed — because he explained the reasoning behind each one rather than just mandating it.

He changed the culture, not just the codebase. That impact will outlast his time with us by years.
