We had been told by two other consultants that migrating our monolith to microservices without downtime was not feasible given our data model. He spent two weeks understanding the system, proposed a strangler fig approach with a shadow-write phase, and executed it over four months with zero production incidents.

More importantly, he documented every decision so we could maintain and evolve the architecture ourselves. We did not just get a migration — we got the knowledge to own what came after it.
