I joined the team as a mid-level engineer and worked directly under his technical leadership for two years. He never gave me the answer. He asked the questions that led me to find it myself — and then made sure I understood why the answer was right, not just that it was.

He reviewed my code with the same rigour he applied to his own, explained every comment, and pushed me to think about systems at a level I had not considered before. After two years working with him, I am a fundamentally better engineer. I think about architecture differently, I write code differently, and I lead technical conversations differently.
